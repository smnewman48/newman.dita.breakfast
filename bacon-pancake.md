<title>Maple Bacon Pancake

<p>You will love this combination of flavors and its baked in a pie plate.

![https://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/8/2011/11/breakfast-becomes-cravetastic.jpg](https://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/8/2011/11/breakfast-becomes-cravetastic.jpg)

<p> Ingredients & Directions

<ul>

<li>4 ounces bacon
<li>1 cup baking mix
<li>1.25 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
<li>1/2 cup milk
<li>1/4 cup maple syrup
<li>2 tablespoons white sugar
<li>2 eggs

<ol>

<li>Preheat oven to 350 degrees
<li>Place bacon in a large, deep skillet
<li>Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown
<li>Drain, crumble and set aside
<li>Spray a 9 inch pie plate with non-stick cooking spray
<li>In a large bowl, beat together baking mix, 3/4 cup cheddar cheese, milk, maple syrup, sugar and eggs until only small lumps of cheese remain
<li>Pour into prepared pie plate
<li>Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of pancake comes out clean
<li>Sprinkle bacon and remaining 1/2 cup cheese over top of pancake
<li>Bake until cheese melts, about 3 to 5 minutes
