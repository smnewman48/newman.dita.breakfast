<title>French Egg and Bacon Sandwich
![https://s-media-cache-ak0.pinimg.com/236x/ea/be/13/eabe13d871df5926be3883b98f2cbcbb.jpg](https://s-media-cache-ak0.pinimg.com/236x/ea/be/13/eabe13d871df5926be3883b98f2cbcbb.jpg)

<p>Ingredients & Directions

<ul>

<li>2 eggs, beaten
<li>4 slices bread
<li>4 slices bacon
<li>2 eggs
<li>1/2 cup maple syrup

<ol>

<li>Dip bread slices in beaten eggs
<li>Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat
<li>Cook until browned on both sides
<li>Set aside but keep warm
<li>Place bacon in a large, deep skillet
<li>Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown
<li>Drain and set aside
<li>Reserve 1 tablespoon of bacon grease in pan and fry remaining two eggs
<li>Place one piece of French toast on each of two plates
<li>Place the fried eggs on top of the bread, top the eggs with strips of bacon
<li>Cover with the remaining pieces of French toast
<li>Following that by pouring on the syrup
