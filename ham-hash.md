<title>Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole


![http://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566024.jpg](http://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566024.jpg)<p>Ingredients & Directions

<ul>

<li>1 32-ounce package frozen hash brown potatoes
<li>8 ounces cooked, diced ham
<li>2 10.75-ounce cans condensed cream of potato soup
<li>1 16-ounce container sour cream
<li>2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
<li>1.5 cups grated Parmesan cheese

<ol>

<li>Preheat oven to 375 degrees
<li>Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish
<li>In a large bowl, mix hash browns, ham, cream of potato soup, sour cream, and Cheddar cheese
<li>Spread evenly into prepared dish
<li>Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
<li>Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until bubbly and lightly brown
<li>Serve immediately
