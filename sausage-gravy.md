<title>Country Sausage Gravy

Ingredients & Directions

<ul>

<li>1 pound pork sausage
<li>1 onion, finely chopped
<li>1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
<li>1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
<li>2 tablespoons garlic, minced <li>4 tablespoons unsalted butter
<li>salt and pepper to taste
<li>4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
<li>1 teaspoon minced fresh sage <li>1 teaspoon minced fresh thyme
<li>2 cups milk, divided
<li>2 cubes chicken bouillon
<li>1/4 cup minced fresh parsley

<ol>

<li>In a skillet on medium heat cook pork, onion, green pepper, red pepper flakes, and garlic until pork is crumbly
<li>Drain off excess fat, but leave a small amount
<li>Combine butter, salt, and pepper with the meat mixture and stir until butter melts
<li>Slowly sift flour over the top
<li>Mix gently and allow mixture to cook for 5 minutes
<li>Add the sage and thyme
<li>Slowly stir in milk, about a half a cup at a time
