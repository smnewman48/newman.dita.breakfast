Vegan Alternative to Bacon-Egg Grilled Cheese

I found a great recipe for bacon-egg grilled cheese, but it doesn't work for vegans. Here's a great alternative.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. soy milk or water
  • salt and pepper, as desired
  • 3 tsp. vegan margarine, room temperature, divided
  • 4 slices of whole wheat or white bread
  • 2 slices of vegan cheese
  • tofu, as desired

Directions

  1. Beat soy milk, salt and pepper in small bowl until blended
  2. Heat 1 tsp. vegan margarine in large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot
  3. Remove from pan and clean skillet
  4. Spread remaining 2 tsp. vegan margarine evenly on one side of each bread slice
  5. Place 2 slices in skillet, buttered side down
  6. Top evenly with vegan cheese and tofu
  7. Cover with remaining bread, buttered side up
  8. Cook sandwiches over medium heat, turning once, until bread is toasted and cheese is melted, 2 to 4 minutes
