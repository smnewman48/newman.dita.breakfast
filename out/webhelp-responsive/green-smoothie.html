Pearrific Green Smoothie >

A smoothie made with fresh veggie juice and blended with a healthy dose of spinach and ripe pear.

Ingredients

  • 2 kale leaves
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 small knob ginger
  • 1 pear, core removed and sliced
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 6-8 ice cubes

Directions

  1. Set juicer setting to 'low', place softer items, like kale, into juicer
  2. Turn to ‘high’ and juice the celery, carrots and ginger
